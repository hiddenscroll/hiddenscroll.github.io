We import and distribute cocoa butter, cocoa powder, cocoa nibs, chocolate chips, white chocolate, caramel, dark chocolate. In twelve months, a single tree can bear 50,000-100,000 cacao blossoms. They produce cocoa pods and cocoa pods have cocoa seeds which are processed to get cocoa powder and cocoa butter.

Reliably processed
